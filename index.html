Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

Welcome to MPD Player – IPTV, M3U & DRM. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your information when you use our media player application.

1. Overview

MPD Player is a media player application. We do not provide, host, or broadcast any media content. All content played through the app is provided by the end-user. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent user experience.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect personal identification information (such as your name, email address, or phone number). However, for basic application functionality, limited technical data is processed:

  • Technical Information: When you access external EPG (Electronic Program Guide) data or load remote playlists, your device's IP Address is temporarily processed to establish a connection with the third-party servers. This data is ephemeral and is not stored or shared by the application for any other purpose.
  • Local Storage: Common access to local storage is requested only to allow users to load and play their own media files and M3U playlists.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide functionality and support the application, we use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services & AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and technical data to serve relevant ads as per their own privacy policies.
  • External EPG & Content Providers: Data is fetched directly from the sources (URLs) provided by the user (e.g., epgshare01).

4. User Responsibilities

Users are solely responsible for the content, URLs, and playlists they use within the application. We do not endorse any specific content and have no affiliation with third-party content providers.

5. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect data processed by the app. Network requests are performed over encrypted HTTPS channels whenever supported by the provider.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
