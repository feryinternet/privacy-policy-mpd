Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

Welcome to MPD Player – IPTV, M3U & DRM. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Overview

MPD Player is a media player application. We do not provide, host, or broadcast any media content. All content played through the app is provided by the end-user. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent user experience.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect personal identification information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) for our own records. However, for the app to function correctly, limited technical data is processed:

  • Technical Information: When you access external EPG (Electronic Program Guide) data or use the Dynamic Wallpaper feature, your device's IP Address is temporarily processed to establish a connection with third-party servers. This data is ephemeral and not stored on our application's servers.
  • Local Storage: The application requests access to local storage to allow you to load and play your own media files and M3U playlists.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide a rich experience and support development, we integrate the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services & AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and technical data (like IP addresses) to serve relevant ads and perform analytics as per their privacy policy.
  • Picsum: Our "Dynamic Wallpaper" feature retrieves high-quality images from Picsum. Using this feature involves connecting to Picsum's servers.
  • External EPG Providers: If you use external EPG links (such as epgshare01), data is fetched directly from those sources.

4. User Responsibilities

Users are fully and solely responsible for the content, URLs, and playlists they input into the application. We do not endorse any specific content provider and have no affiliation with any third-party content services.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the data processed by our app. All outgoing network requests for content fetching are performed over encrypted HTTPS channels whenever available.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
